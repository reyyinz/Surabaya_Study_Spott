About Us

The Story Behind

Many of our friends, Surabaya students, have to face laptops for a long time to complete their assignments or study, this makes many students often stressed and burn out, one of the reasons is because the room atmosphere is always the same and boring.

Vision and Mission

We want to help students overcome boredom in their surroundings by providing an inspiring and refreshing learning environment. To achieve this, we have developed a website that offers a choice of convenient and conducive locations, suitable for completing tasks. and stay productive.
